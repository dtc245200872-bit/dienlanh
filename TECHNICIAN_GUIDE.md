# Technician Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 Main Features

### 1. Technician Dashboard
**Access**: Main navigation menu → "Bảng điều khiển" or direct URL: `/Repair/TechnicianDashboard`

**What You'll See:**
- **Quick Stats**: 
  - Pending assignments waiting for you
  - Jobs completed today
  - Jobs completed this month
  - Your average customer rating
  
- **Assigned Tasks Section**: 
  - Shows up to 5 most recent pending jobs
  - Quick view of device type, issue, address, and scheduled time
  - Click "Accept Job" to start working
  - Click "View Details" for more information

- **Work History Section**: 
  - Your last 10 completed jobs
  - Quick reference for recent work

- **Profile Card**: 
  - Your name and contact information
  - Professional specializations
  - Work status (Ready/Busy)

- **Schedule Card**: 
  - Your work hours
  - Total jobs completed

- **Notifications**: 
  - Any unread notifications
  - Latest system messages

---

### 2. Assigned Tasks
**Access**: Navigation menu → "Công việc" → "Công việc được phân công" or `/Repair/AssignedTasks`

**What You Can Do:**
- View ALL pending assignments (not just 5)
- See priority badges:
  - 🔴 **Urgent** - Job scheduled within 2 hours
  - 🟢 **Normal** - Regular priority

- For Each Task:
  - Device type and issue description
  - Customer's address and contact info
  - Scheduled date and time
  - Device photo (if provided)
  - **Accept Job**: Click to start working
  - **View Details**: See full information in a popup

---

### 3. Work History
**Access**: Navigation menu → "Công việc" → "Lịch sử công việc" or `/Repair/WorkHistory`

**What You'll See:**
- Complete list of all your completed work
- Status indicators:
  - 🔵 In Progress
  - ✅ Completed
  - 💳 Paid
  - ⚠️ Issue Reported

- Customer Ratings:
  - ⭐ Star ratings
  - 💬 Customer comments
  - If no rating yet: "Not yet rated"

- Actions:
  - Click "View" to see full job details
  - See replaced parts
  - View final cost

---

### 4. Personal Profile
**Access**: Navigation menu → "Hồ sơ" or `/Repair/Profile`

**Edit Your Information:**

**Basic Info:**
- Full name
- Phone number
- Address
- Specializations (areas of expertise)
  - Example: Điều hòa, Tủ lạnh, Máy giặt (Air conditioning, Refrigerator, Washing machine)

**Work Schedule:**
- Start time (e.g., 08:00)
- End time (e.g., 19:00)
- Toggle: "I'm ready to receive assignments"

**Your Statistics:**
- Total jobs completed
- Average customer rating
- Current availability status

**How to Update:**
1. Fill in the form fields
2. Click "Save Changes"
3. You'll be redirected to dashboard

---

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### For Better Job Assignments:
1. Keep your profile updated with specializations
2. Set accurate work hours
3. Toggle your availability when you can accept jobs
4. Accept jobs promptly - faster acceptance = more assignments

### For Better Ratings:
1. Arrive on time to scheduled appointments
2. Communicate with customers clearly
3. Do quality work on first visit
4. Document work with photos
5. Follow up after completion

### Managing Your Workload:
1. Check "Assigned Tasks" regularly for new jobs
2. Accept jobs you can handle within the scheduled time
3. Use work history to track patterns
4. Update your work schedule if needed

---

## ⏰ Job Acceptance Workflow

1. **Dashboard or Assigned Tasks**: View pending work
2. **Review Details**: Click the job to see full information
3. **Accept Job**: Click "Accept" to start working
   - Status changes to "In Progress"
   - It appears in your work history
   - Customer is notified
4. **Complete Work**: Update job status when finished
5. **Await Payment**: Once customer confirms payment, you can see final ratings

| Metric | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Công việc chờ nhận | New jobs assigned to you but not yet accepted |
| Hoàn thành hôm nay | Jobs you finished today |
| Hoàn thành tháng này | Jobs you finished this calendar month |

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Can't see assigned tasks?**
- Make sure your availability is toggled "ON"
- Check that your work hours are correctly set
- Log out and log back in

**Low ratings?**
- Review customer feedback in Work History
- Address common issues customers mention
- Consider taking a skills refresher course

**Can't update profile?**
- Click "Save Changes" button
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check for error messages
